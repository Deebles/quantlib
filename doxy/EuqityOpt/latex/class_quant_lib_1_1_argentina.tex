\subsection{Detailed Description}
Argentinian calendars. 

Holidays for the Buenos Aires stock exchange (data from {\tt http\+://www.\+merval.\+sba.\+com.\+ar/})\+: 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Saturdays 
\item Sundays 
\item New Year\textquotesingle{}s Day, January 1st 
\item Holy Thursday 
\item Good Friday 
\item Labour Day, May 1st 
\item May Revolution, May 25th 
\item Death of General Manuel Belgrano, third Monday of June 
\item Independence Day, July 9th 
\item Death of General José de San Martín, third Monday of August 
\item Columbus Day, October 12th (moved to preceding Monday if on Tuesday or Wednesday and to following if on Thursday or Friday) 
\item Immaculate Conception, December 8th 
\item Christmas Eve, December 24th 
\item New Year\textquotesingle{}s Eve, December 31th 
\end{DoxyItemize}
