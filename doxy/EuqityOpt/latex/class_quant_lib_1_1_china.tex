Chinese calendar. 

Holidays\+: 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Saturdays 
\item Sundays 
\item New Year\textquotesingle{}s day, January 1st (possibly followed by one or two more holidays) 
\item Labour Day, first week in May 
\item National Day, one week from October 1st 
\end{DoxyItemize}
