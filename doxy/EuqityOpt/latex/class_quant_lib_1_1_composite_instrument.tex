\subsection{Detailed Description}
Composite instrument 

This instrument is an aggregate of other instruments. Its N\+PV is the sum of the N\+P\+Vs of its components, each possibly multiplied by a given factor.

{\bfseries Example\+: } \doxyref{static replication of a down-\/and-\/out barrier option }{p.}{}

\begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
Methods that drive the calculation directly (such as recalculate(), freeze() and others) might not work correctly. 
\end{DoxyWarning}
