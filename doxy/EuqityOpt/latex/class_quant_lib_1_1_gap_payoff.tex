\subsection{Detailed Description}
Binary gap payoff. 

This payoff is equivalent to being a) long a \doxyref{Plain\+Vanilla\+Payoff}{p.}{class_quant_lib_1_1_plain_vanilla_payoff} at the first strike (same Call/\+Put type) and b) short a \doxyref{Cash\+Or\+Nothing\+Payoff}{p.}{class_quant_lib_1_1_cash_or_nothing_payoff} at the first strike (same Call/\+Put type) with cash payoff equal to the difference between the second and the first strike. \begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
this payoff can be negative depending on the strikes 
\end{DoxyWarning}
