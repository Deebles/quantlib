\subsection{Detailed Description}
Year-\/on-\/year inflation-\/indexed swap. 

Quoted as a fixed rate $ K $. At start\+: \[ \sum_{i=1}^{M} P_n(0,t_i) N K = \sum_{i=1}^{M} P_n(0,t_i) N \left[ \frac{I(t_i)}{I(t_i-1)} - 1 \right] \] where $ t_M $ is the maturity time, $ P_n(0,t) $ is the nominal discount factor at time $ t $, $ N $ is the notional, and $ I(t) $ is the inflation index value at time $ t $.

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
These instruments have now been changed to follow typical \doxyref{Vanilla\+Swap}{p.}{class_quant_lib_1_1_vanilla_swap} type design conventions w.\+r.\+t. Schedules etc. 
\end{DoxyNote}
